The Challenge

Customers came for help we couldn't give

Customers walked into uBreakiFix by Asurion stores expecting help with their Asurion claims. But stores couldn't assist‚Äîregulatory constraints meant employees couldn't file claims on customers' behalf (they aren't insurance adjustors, and doing so could constitute fraud), and technical limitations meant store systems didn't connect to Asurion's claims systems.

The result? Technicians spent too much time trying to help anyway, stores turned customers away and asked them to go home to file online, or sent them to carrier stores for help. It was a frustrating, negative experience for everyone.

The Approach

Service blueprints to align stakeholders at odds

We started by mapping the service experience with store employees, operations, and the claims product team using service blueprints. This helped us identify pain points and opportunities‚Äîand got stakeholders who were initially at odds aligned on a shared understanding of the problem.

The ideal experience we identified: give customers an easy way to file and manage claims without compliance issues by warmly handing them off to an in-store tablet for self-service.
