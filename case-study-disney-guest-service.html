60+ apps, paper documents, and frustrated Cast Members

Disney staff struggled with efficiency issues helping guests in the parks‚Äîcausing guest frustration and complaints. Cast Members had to navigate multiple applications, use paper documents, and rely on manual processes to assist guests pre-arrival, on-site, and post-visit.

The Solution

One responsive app to replace them all

We combined functionality from several legacy applications into four main sections: Guest Interaction Tracking, Guest Itinerary & Scheduling, Tickets & Media, and Admin. The interface works seamlessly across devices, from desktop to mobile.
